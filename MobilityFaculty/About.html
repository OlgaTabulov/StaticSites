MobilityFaculty
Olga combines her expertise in Functional Movement, Fascial Stretch Therapy, Yoga Teacher Training and 20 years of being athletically active in weightlifting, powerlifting, circuit training, high intensity interval training, cardio training, calisthenics, supersets, yoga, mobility training, movement culture.

She brings the best of these practices into a method that develops a body in a well-rounded, progressive and healthy way.

She teaches her clients how to make real and lasting changes in their range of motion, contributing to overall health and well-being.
